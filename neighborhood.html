Columbus, Ohio

Columbus is the capital of Ohio. The city is fairly easy to get around since it has grown outwards in the suburbs over the last several decades. Recently there was a movement to rename the city "Flavortown". In honor of that nickname here are some good restaraunts:

  • Morettis
  • Gallos
  • Del Mar
  • Lupo
  • CBC
  • Knotty Pine
  • Cap City
  • Fish Market
